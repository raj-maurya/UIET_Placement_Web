Placement Cell:

A Placement Cell has been established to start placement services and organization of summer training. Our graduates can be found working in reputed companies both in the public as well as in private sectors like Defence Research and Development Organization (DRDO), Steel Authority of India Ltd. (SAIL), Hindustan Lever Limited (HLL), Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), Infosys, HCL, Oswal Chemicals, Scooter India Ltd, Lohia Starlinger, Samsung Electronics, Super House, Goodlass Nerolac Paints, Koshika Telecom Ltd, NTPC, Hughes Software Systems, IBM etc.

A substantial number of our students have been securing very high percentiles every year in the GATE Examination and have joined several prestigious institutions like different IITs, IISc Bangalore, NITIE Mumbai and ISM Dhanbad for higher studies. Some of our students are also pursuing their higher education at the Universities in the USA and UK. Every year, we also find a few students taking up teaching jobs in various colleges and there are some who diversify and join various management programs. UIET can boast of a few brilliant students who have presented papers in international conferences held in prestigious institutes like MIT, USA. Students of such caliber will help UIET to grow into an institute of international repute.
