Why recruit @ UIET?

T&P Head

"To organize campus interviews for final year students with industries and business houses of repute from all over India. To prepare students to face campus interviews by arranging training in Aptitude tests, group discussions, preparing for Technical and HR interviews through professional trainers. To promote career counseling by organising guidance lectures by senior corporate personnel and most importantly by the immediately placed senior students."
